About me

How I Got Interest in Computer Field?

Hello! My name is Pawan Bafila, and I am from Dev Bhoomi Uttarakhand, India. My journey into the world of graphic and web design began when I was in the 11th grade. I took a computer class where I was introduced to C programming. Although it was a bit challenging at first, it sparked my curiosity about the vast possibilities within the tech world.

After completing my 12th grade, I started observing how companies from China and the USA were building their empires through their applications and achieving near-monopolies. This realization made me reflect on the state of technology in my own country. I noticed a gap: while many were providing services, few were striving to create groundbreaking applications.

Driven by a desire to contribute to my country's technological landscape, I developed a strong interest in web designing and development. My goal is to create impactful applications, akin to giants like YouTube and Facebook, that can revolutionize the way we interact with technology.

I have since pursued my passion by earning a diploma in Graphic and Web Designing from Attitude Academy, Yamuna Vihar, Delhi. My technical skills include HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, Figma, Photoshop,illustrator,canva, CorelDraw, WordPress, Git, and GitHub. I have worked on various projects, including creating clone home pages of Amazon, Flipkart, and Netflix, all of which are hosted on GitHub and netlify
